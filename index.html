Welcome to Redports

It does not matter if you are a FreeBSD ports maintainer or committer or you are just about to create your first FreeBSD port - redports helps you to test your port on various FreeBSD versions.

Getting started with Redports is simple and all you need is a GitHub account and a new repository. Don't worry you can also use Subversion. https://help.github.com/articles/support-for-subversion-clients/

Authors and Contributors

Maintaining the redports service is a time consuming task and various FreeBSD developers and contributors are helping to keep it alive. We are always searching for new people to get involved! Just get in contact with us via IRC #redports on freenode.
